Meet Emilio, he loves visiting his "Abuela" (Mexican Grandmother)
Why? Because she has the best garden.
It has lots of flowers and trees.
Emilio also likes to play with Abuela's dog Pipo.
Together, Emilio and Pipo try to fly like the hummingbirds that visit Abuela's birdbath.
Together, they like to sing like the crickets that live in the coriander patch.
Together, they try to catch the butterflies that sit on top of Abuela's tall sunflowers.
Together, they like to dig up the dirt underneath the avocado tree to find earthworms.
And sometimes, Pipo even digs up some bones he buried earlier to snack on!
Best of all, after playing in the garden Abuela makes yummy tacos for Emilio to eat too!
